Who are we

INSPECTION-CHINA is a third-party quality services company. We provide a comprehensive range of quality inspection, factory audit, and technical consultation services, leveraging our professional expertise. During inspections and audits, all client instructions are strictly followed. We deliver accurate and reliable first-hand information to our clients, serving as their eyes and ears.

In addition to quality inspection and audit services, our sourcing and trade assistance are also highly valued by our clients---particularly import companies and entrepreneurs. With an experienced team supporting every step, we ensure our clients enjoy a smooth and worry-free purchasing and production process in China.

Our customized solutions are designed to ensure that products meet industry standards, are manufactured according to client specifications, and fulfill consumer expectations.

As an independent third-party service provider, INSPECTION-CHINA acts as your extended Quality Assurance and Technical team in China. We consistently collaborate with our clients, offering consultation and working alongside them throughout the process.

Why choose us

Our success is built upon a dual foundation: the steadfast trust and partnership of our clients, and the deeply held core values that guide every member of our team. These principles---integrity, excellence, and collaborative success---not only shape our internal culture but also directly translate into the reliable, high-quality service we deliver. It is this synergy between external confidence and internal commitment that fuels our continuous growth and achievement.

Industry Expertise

The INSPECTION-CHINA team possesses extensive expertise across diverse sectors, including machinery, textiles, footwear, plastics, furniture, kitchenware, home appliances, toys, fashion, stationery, and promotional products. We provide targeted recommendations from the initial stage of engagement. Our engineers are adept at developing efficient, practical solutions tailored to the specific conditions of your project.

Transparency

INSPECTION-CHINA maintains full transparency in all supplier communications. Clients are promptly updated following any discussion with suppliers. We immediately report any irregularities identified during inspections or routine communications, ensuring no information is withheld between our team and the client.

Timing

INSPECTION-CHINA strictly adheres to all agreed service timelines. Delays in operations or report delivery are not permitted. We provide detailed inspection reports---complete with photos and videos---within 24 hours. For urgent requests, a comprehensive report can be delivered within 8 hours of task completion.

Linguistic Skill

In addition to English, our standard working language, INSPECTION-CHINA has team members fluent in Japanese, French, and Italian. This multilingual capability ensures clear, seamless communication between clients and suppliers, eliminating any language barriers throughout the process.

Confidentiality

Protecting business and industrial secrets is a fundamental principle at INSPECTION-CHINA. Our team strictly upholds this commitment before, during, and after service delivery. Client information, product details, supplier contacts, and trade data are never disclosed to any third party.

Reliability

INSPECTION-CHINA guarantees that no undisclosed agreements are made with suppliers, and we enforce strict procedures to prevent commercial bribery. In trade assistance services, we derive no profit from client-supplier transactions. Our compensation is solely the service fee paid by the client, ensuring impartial and trustworthy support.

Inspection Services

Our inspection services provide comprehensive quality control throughout your supply chain. We ensure that every product meets your specifications and quality standards before shipment.

Our Inspection Process

1. Pre-Production Inspection: Verify raw materials and production readiness before manufacturing begins.

2. During Production Inspection: Monitor production processes to identify issues early and ensure quality control.

3. Final Random Inspection: Check finished products against your specifications before shipment.

4. Container Loading Supervision: Ensure correct products are loaded in proper condition and quantity.

Benefits of Our Inspection Services

  • Early detection of quality issues
  • Reduced risk of receiving defective products
  • Improved supplier performance
  • Detailed reports with photos and videos
  • Timely delivery of inspection results

Audit Services

Our factory audit services help you evaluate potential suppliers and ensure they meet your standards for quality, ethics, and reliability.

Types of Audits We Offer

3. Social Compliance Audit: Ensure factories comply with labor laws, safety standards, and ethical practices.

Audit Benefits
